FAQ

Common Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. You can cancel at any time from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of the billing period — no mid-cycle charges.
What happens to my progress if I downgrade?
Your progress is always saved and never deleted. Certificates you've already earned remain valid forever. You just won't access premium-only courses until you resubscribe.
Is my payment information safe?
Yes. In a production deployment, all payments would be processed through a certified payment provider (such as PayMongo for GCash/Maya/cards in the Philippines). Card numbers would never touch SkillPace's own servers — they'd be tokenized securely by the provider.
Can I upgrade from Semester to Annual mid-plan?
Yes! You can upgrade at any time. We'll apply a prorated credit for the remaining Semester period toward your Annual plan automatically.
